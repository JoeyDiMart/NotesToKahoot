Q: What type of attack involves an attacker intercepting communication between two parties to steal or modify data?
A: Phishing
B: Man-in-the-Middle (MitM)
C: SQL Injection
D: Cross-Site Scripting (XSS)
CORRECT: B
---
Q: Which of the following is a symmetric encryption algorithm?
A: RSA
B: Diffie-Hellman
C: AES
D: ECC
CORRECT: C

Q: What is the primary purpose of a honeypot in network security?
A: To encrypt sensitive data
B: To detect and analyze attacks by attracting malicious actors
C: To filter incoming network traffic
D: To store backup copies of critical files
CORRECT: B
---
Q: Which authentication factor is represented by something you know?
A: Fingerprint scan
B: Smart card
C: Password
D: Security token
CORRECT: C

Q: What does the principle of least privilege mean in security?
A: Users should have the minimum level of access necessary to perform their job
B: All users should have administrator privileges
C: Passwords should be changed as infrequently as possible
D: Security policies should be kept confidential
CORRECT: A
