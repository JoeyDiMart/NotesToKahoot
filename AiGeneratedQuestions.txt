Q: What is a "clone phishing" attack?
A: Creating a fake login page mimicking a real site
B: Duplicating a legitimate email with malicious links
C: Registering a domain that looks like a trusted brand
D: Sending phishing emails from a cloned mail server
CORRECT: B
